How scoring works Quick guide

Each comparison uses weighted stat categories. The active lens decides which skills matter most.

  • Scoring, Shooting, Playmaking, Defense, and Efficiency each focus on different production.
  • Position Adjusted changes the weights based on the player role or position.
  • Use games played and minutes filters to avoid tiny samples.
  • No-stats players can appear in search and Finder, but they are blocked from comparisons.
